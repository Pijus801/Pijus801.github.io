About me:

I was born in Lithuania, and came to the US in 2000. Ever since I was little I've been drawing, and filling sketch books until they were overflowing with art. My favorite type of art is realist, particularly portraits done in stipple. There's something really relaxing about the repetition of it.
I'm currently 22 years of age, and refuse to quit drawing and creating art. All of my pieces can have prints made if you're interested in purchasing them, please contact me via direct message through any of the social media outlets below.

All art was created originally by myself, however, the images were hosted by the website: Instagram
